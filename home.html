Frequently Asked Questions - Farm Ferry

What is Farm Ferry?
Farm Ferry is a B2B platform providing fresh farm produce and kitchen essentials directly to restaurants and businesses.
Where does Farm Ferry source its products?
We source directly from certified farmers and suppliers to ensure premium quality and freshness.
Who can order from Farm Ferry?
Only verified restaurants, hotels, and food service providers can place bulk orders on Farm Ferry.
How can I place an order on Farm Ferry?
Log in with your registered business account, select products, and confirm the quantity to place your order.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it via the dashboard.
What is the delivery time?
Orders are delivered within 24–48 hours depending on your city and order volume.
What is the refund policy?
Refunds are provided only for damaged or missing items reported within 6 hours of delivery.
How long does it take to receive a refund?
Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.
Will I get a full refund including delivery charges?
Yes, if the issue is valid and reported on time, you’ll receive a full refund including delivery charges.
How do I register for a Farm Ferry account?
Click on “Sign Up”, provide your business details, and complete mobile OTP verification to register.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
Click “Forgot Password” on the login page to receive a reset link via mobile or email.
Can I update my business profile information?
Yes, you can update your business name, address, and GST details in the profile settings.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit cards, and Wallet payments.
Is Cash on Delivery available?
No, Farm Ferry supports only prepaid online payments for operational efficiency.
Will I receive an invoice for my order?
Yes, a digital GST invoice is generated and sent after each successful transaction.
